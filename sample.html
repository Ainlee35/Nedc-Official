About NEDC

NEDC is a membership-based institution established to champion entrepreneurship, enterprise growth, and innovation in Tanzania. We ensure that no idea, no talent, and no dream is wasted.

Vision

A Tanzania where every entrepreneur has the opportunity, resources, and support to build enterprises that create jobs and drive innovation.

Mission

Close the gap between the haves and have-nots by connecting entrepreneurs to finance, markets, skills, and policy.

Flagship Programs

BongoFursa TV Pitch Show

A national televised show where entrepreneurs pitch to investors and secure mentorship and funding.

Innovation Hub

A co-working and incubation hub where startups can test ideas and grow.

Youth Insurance

An innovative safety net protecting youth entrepreneurs from shocks and risks.
