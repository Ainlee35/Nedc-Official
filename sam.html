Our Flagship Programs

Driving entrepreneurship, innovation, and inclusive growth across Tanzania.

BongoFursa TV Pitch Show

A televised national business pitch show connecting entrepreneurs with investors, mentors, and partners. It provides visibility for innovative startups and facilitates deal flow to strengthen Tanzania’s entrepreneurial ecosystem.

BongoFursa App

A digital platform that offers entrepreneurs access to mentorship, financing opportunities, markets, and networking. It ensures continuous engagement and resource sharing beyond physical events.

Innovation Hub

A physical co-working and incubation space designed to nurture startups, provide prototyping facilities, and connect innovators with business advisory services and funding partners.

Youth Insurance

An innovative financial safety net for young entrepreneurs, offering affordable insurance products to safeguard them against risks, shocks, and business uncertainties.

National Innovation Awards

An annual award program recognizing outstanding entrepreneurs, startups, and innovators whose contributions drive socio-economic transformation in Tanzania.

BLY (Business Literacy for Youth) Syllabus

A structured curriculum developed to instill business literacy among youth in secondary schools, colleges, and vocational institutions, preparing them for enterprise creation and management.

From Idea to Business Program

A training and mentorship initiative that helps aspiring entrepreneurs convert raw ideas into viable business ventures by equipping them with essential skills and support.
