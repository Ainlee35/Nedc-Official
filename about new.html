About Us

The National Enterprise and Development Chamber (NEDC) is a membership-based institution established to champion entrepreneurship, enterprise growth, and innovation in Tanzania.NEDC was founded with a clear mission: to close the gap in our entrepreneurial ecosystem and create an environment where no idea, no talent, and no dream is wasted. Across Tanzania, we see young people, women, and men with extraordinary energy and creativit

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To become Africa’s premier platform for uniting visionary entrepreneurs and high-impact investors in a redefined, trusted, and transformative environment.

Mission

NEDC is committed to accelerating economic growth by bridging the gap between enterprise and capital through strategic networking, premium events, and world-class advisory support.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of trust, ethics, and transparency.

Innovation

We foster creativity and embrace new ideas to solve pressing challenges.

Collaboration

We believe in teamwork and strategic partnerships to create sustainable impact.

Excellence

We strive for quality and impact in everything we do.

Sustainability

We are committed to lasting solutions that benefit future generations.

Accountability

We take full responsibility for our actions and outcomes.

Our Strategic Pillars

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Building an ecosystem that supports startups and innovators to grow sustainably.

Finance & Investment

Bridging the gap between enterprises and investors for inclusive economic growth.

Capacity Building

Providing training, mentorship, and advisory services to entrepreneurs and SMEs.

Partnerships & Networks

Creating trusted collaborations with government, donors, and private sector partners.

Our Commitment to Sustainable Development Goals

Gender Equality

Ensuring at least 40% of our programs benefit women, with specific initiatives for women-led SMEs

Good Health & Well-Being

Supporting innovations in health enterprises and ensuring decent working conditions

Climate Action&Life before water

Promoting green businesses,renewable energy, and blue economy entrepreneurship.

Decent Work & Economic Growth

We formalize MSMEs and expand access to finance to promote inclusive and sustainable growth.

Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

We create hubs for innovation, where technology and enterprise connect to build sustainable businesses.

Partnerships for the Goals

Building strong alliances with local and global partners.
