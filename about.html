About NEDC

National Enterprise & Development Chamber

Who We Are

NEDC is Tanzania’s premier platform for uniting entrepreneurs, investors, and stakeholders to accelerate economic transformation. We bridge the gap between enterprise and capital through trusted networks, advisory support, and impactful initiatives. Our mission is to ensure no idea, dream, or talent is wasted — instead, they are nurtured into sustainable ventures.

Vision

To become Africa’s premier platform for uniting visionary entrepreneurs and high-impact investors in a redefined, trusted, and transformative environment.

Mission

NEDC is committed to accelerating economic growth by bridging the gap between enterprise and capital through strategic networking, premium events, and world-class advisory support.

What We Do

Trade & Investment Support

We connect enterprises to high-impact investors and open new trade opportunities for sustainable growth.

Entrepreneurship Development

Through mentorship, training, and advisory services, we nurture entrepreneurs to scale their businesses.

Access to Capital

We bridge the financing gap by linking enterprises with investors and seed capital providers.
