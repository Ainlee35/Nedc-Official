    About NEDC

    The National Enterprise and Development Chamber (NEDC) drives innovation by connecting entrepreneurs, startups, and enterprises across Tanzania and beyond. We champion bold ideas and strong relationships, offering support at every level—from local communities to national platforms. Focused on startups and fresh innovations, NEDC provides resources, guidance, and networks to turn ideas into impact. As a trusted voice in enterprise development, we empower micro, small, and medium businesses and help shape an inclusive, sustainable economic future. At the National Enterprise and Development Chamber (NEDC), we provide practical solutions and strategic support to build strong, sustainable enterprises across Tanzania and beyond. Our mission is to turn potential into performance by unlocking access to resources, building entrepreneurial capacity, and driving inclusive growth. We work with startups, SMEs, youth, investors, and ecosystem partners to ensure that innovation leads to real impact. Here’s how we make it happen:

    Our Vision & Mission

    Vision

    To become Africa’s premier platform for uniting visionary entrepreneurs and high-impact investors in a redefined, trusted, and transformative environment.

    Mission

    NEDC is committed to accelerating economic growth by bridging the gap between enterprise and capital through strategic networking, premium events, and world-class advisory support.
